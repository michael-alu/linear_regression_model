# AI Job Market Salary Predictor

## Mission

My mission is to lift Africa out of poverty by educating and inspiring the young generation, equipping them with in-demand skills required to earn them an income — and creating a foundation that will continue to do this even after I die.

This project is a step toward that mission. It seeks to understand what drives income in the AI industry so aspiring professionals can make informed career decisions.

## Dataset

**Source**

[Kaggle — Global AI Job Market & Salary Trends 2025](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/bismasajjad/global-ai-job-market-and-salary-trends-2025)

**Author**

Bisma Sajjad

**Description**

This comprehensive dataset contains detailed information about AI and machine learning job positions, salaries, and market trends across different countries, experience levels, and company sizes. Perfect for data science enthusiasts, career researchers, and market analysts for practice purposes.

**Size**

15,000 rows across 19 original columns, expanded to 61 features after feature engineering.
